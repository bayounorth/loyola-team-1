What is This Program?

description

About

This is where innovation starts. In this ten-week “bootcamp,” you’ll earn more than a certificate that you can put on your resume. You will immerse yourself in a new discipline and build a foundation for a rewarding and multifaceted career in technology. At Loyola, you will learn in-demand local skills from local practitioners. Through a mix of coursework and hands-on implementation, this course will help you develop your own personal portfolio of web projects.

class

What's the Curriculum?

In this program, students will learn to build responsive websites from the ground up using HTML & CSS, as well as making them interactive with Javascript. Other topics include classes on various frameworks such as BootStrap and AngularJs. Students will also gain a deep understanding of backend technologies and databases. By the end of the program, students will be able to build a fully functioning web application that they can show off to future employers, as well as other smaller projects to fill out their portfolio.

school

What's the Goal?

The ultimate goal of this program is to teach recent graduates the fundamental skills that they will need in order to get a job in the tech industry. This program serves as a stepping stone for those that want to find a job in this industry in New Orleans by immersing them in all things related to the industry, as well as taking them to several prominent tech companies in the city through weekly job site visits.
